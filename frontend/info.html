Prevention Tips

Proper Plant Spacing

Ensure adequate spacing between plants to improve air circulation and reduce humidity that can promote disease development.

Water Management

Water plants at the base to keep foliage dry. Avoid overhead watering which can splash pathogens onto leaves.

Crop Rotation

Rotate crops annually to break disease cycles and prevent soil-borne pathogens from building up in the soil.

Clean Tools

Sanitize gardening tools between plants and remove infected plant material to prevent disease spread.

Regular Monitoring

Inspect plants weekly for early signs of disease. Early detection allows for more effective treatment.

Resistant Varieties

Choose disease-resistant plant varieties when available to reduce the likelihood of infection.
